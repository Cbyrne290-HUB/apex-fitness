How It Started

TIRED OF ORDINARY GYMS

Apex Fitness was founded in 2019 by strength coach and former competitive athlete Conor Reilly. After spending years training in commercial gyms that prioritised quantity over quality, Conor decided to build the facility he always wanted but could never find.

The vision was simple — a serious training environment with premium equipment, expert coaches and a community built on accountability and real results. No gimmicks, no fluff, just training done properly.

Five years later Apex has grown into one of Dublin's most respected fitness facilities with over 2,400 members and a coaching team that has helped hundreds of people completely transform their health and performance.

The Facility

WORLD CLASS EQUIPMENT

Strength Zone

12,000 sq ft of dedicated strength training space with premium barbells, power racks, dumbbells up to 100kg and specialty bars.

Cardio Suite

State of the art cardio equipment including Assault bikes, ski ergs, rowing machines and a 40m indoor sprint track.

Group Studio

A dedicated 3,000 sq ft group fitness studio with sprung flooring, full AV system and capacity for 40 participants per class.

Recovery Centre

Ice baths, infrared sauna, compression therapy and a full stretch area to help you recover faster and train harder.
